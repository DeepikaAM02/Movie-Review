My top 3 movies of all-time

Chithha

The performances, too, are top-notch. There are also a couple of tender moments amidst all the grimness. It is such profound moments that elevate Chithha into something vital in these times.

Viduthalai

Viduthalai Part 1 maintains a consistent pace and keeps viewers engaged with moments of high tension.

Mark Antony

Mark Antony is not a great film, but it's quite a refreshing entertainer that delivers what it promises.
